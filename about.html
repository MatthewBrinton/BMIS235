About SpongeBob

Who is SpongeBob?

SpongeBob SquarePants is a cartoon character who has been a sensation since 1999. SpongeBob is a frycook for the restaurant Krusty Krab, who lives in Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob teaches us that a good attitude and a little bit of hard work can go a long way.

SpongeBob Fun Facts

  • SpongeBob’s favorite hobbies include jellyfishing, blowing bubbles, and singing.
  • His best friend is Patrick Star, a dim-witted yet lovable pink starfish.
  • SpongeBob has a pet snail named Gary who meows like a cat.
  • He was inspired by a sponge from a dishwashing sponge commercial.
  • SpongeBob is known for his optimistic catchphrase: “I’m ready, I’m ready!”

SpongeBob's Adventures

From working at the Krusty Krab to saving Bikini Bottom from the town villain, Plankton, SpongeBob always has exciting adventures. Whether he's jellyfishing with his best friend Patrick, trying to earn his drivers license (boating license), or hosting a rock concert, SpongeBob is always ready.

SpongeBob's Friends

  • Patrick Star: SpongeBob’s best friend and fellow adventurer. While Patrick may not be the sharpest tool in the shed, his loyalty to SpongeBob knows no bounds.
  • Squidward Tentacles: SpongeBob’s grumpy neighbor who works with him at the Krusty Krab. Though Squidward often prefers solitude, he can’t escape the constant energy of SpongeBob and Patrick.
  • Sandy Cheeks: A squirrel from Texas who lives underwater in a high-tech dome. She’s an inventor, scientist, and one of SpongeBob’s closest allies.
  • Mr. Krabs: The money-loving owner of the Krusty Krab who is constantly trying to make a profit. Though he can be greedy, he deeply cares for SpongeBob.
